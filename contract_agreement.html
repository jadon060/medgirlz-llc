Agreement Terms

This Provider Contract Agreement (“Agreement”) is made between: 1. MedGirlz LLC (“Company”), and 2. The undersigned provider/contractor (“Provider”). Purpose. MedGirlz LLC supports clinical, wellness, and care-related services under the MedGirlz brand. Provider wishes to deliver services under MedGirlz LLC and receive Company support, access, and branding. Term. The initial term of this Agreement is THREE (3) MONTHS beginning on the Start Date listed above (“Initial Trial Term”). During this 3-month period, the parties will evaluate workload, number of clients, and time required. Compensation. • If Provider serves fewer than ten (10) active clients under MedGirlz LLC, the compensation to Provider is $3,500.00 per month. • If Provider serves ten (10) or more active clients under MedGirlz LLC, the compensation to Provider is $5,000.00 per month. • “Active clients” means clients currently being coordinated, staffed, or serviced through MedGirlz LLC under Provider’s caseload. Review / Renegotiation. At the conclusion of the 3-month Initial Trial Term, the parties will meet to review performance, workload, and program growth. At that time, MedGirlz LLC and Provider may negotiate higher pay, more hours, or expanded duties based on need and availability. Duties of Provider. Provider agrees to: • Represent MedGirlz LLC professionally with families, partners, and clients; • Follow all MedGirlz LLC policies, forms, and documentation standards; • Communicate promptly and maintain schedule expectations; • Protect confidential information and client records. Duties of Company. MedGirlz LLC agrees to: • Provide branding, forms, and administrative guidance similar to the ESS model; • Maintain centralized record-keeping; • Offer reasonable onboarding, communication, and support for Provider. Independent Contractor. Provider is an independent contractor, not an employee. Provider is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and compliance. Termination. Either party may terminate this Agreement with written notice if the other party fails to perform, or at the end of the 3-month trial if either party decides not to continue. By signing below, both parties agree to the terms of this Agreement.
