About Us

At MedGirlz LLC, we believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive in their own unique way. Our work is rooted in person-centered support, which means we put the voice, choice, and goals of the person at the center of everything we do. We listen first, then design supports that reflect what is important to and important for the person.

Whether it’s developing daily living skills, connecting with the community, or building independence, we tailor our services to meet the specific needs of each participant. We recognize that every journey is different, and our role is to walk alongside individuals and families to make sure services are not just provided, but are meaningful, respectful, and empowering.

MedGirlz LLC is committed to flexibility, reliability, and collaboration. We partner with families, facilitators, and community agencies to ensure services are aligned with the person’s life vision. Our mission is simple: supporting people to live their best lives, their way.

Our Services

CPR

Hands-on, practical CPR instruction to help staff and families respond confidently in emergencies. Training follows current safety standards.

Medication Trainer

Guidance on safe medication administration, documentation, and caregiver education to keep clients supported and compliant.

CPI (Crisis Prevention)

De-escalation methods and person-centered strategies to keep everyone safe while honoring dignity and respect.

Nutritionist

Basic nutrition education tailored to client needs — promoting healthy choices, meal planning, and overall wellness.
